Aster

 
        
    When they bloom: Mid-summer to fall

    Why we love them: 
     It's one of our favorite perennials 
     for a reason! Aster blooms for
     a long period, adding color just as
     all the other flowers are winding 
     down for the season.

Allium

 

     When it blooms: Late spring
    
     Why we love it:
        Tall, globe-shaped flowers 
        (some are as big as your hand!)
        are a striking addition to 
        the spring garden.Rodents won't
         bother these bulbs.
